// 02 — Workflow

Procedure

01
Source Video Selection
Identified and acquired the live-action footage to be used as the base layer. Reviewed frame composition and subject movement for rotoscoping feasibility.
02
Frame-by-Frame Tracing
Used rotoscoping tools to manually trace the subject outline across each frame, creating a clean mask that isolates the live-action subject from the background.
03
Layer Compositing
Combined the masked live-action layer with animated and illustrated elements, ensuring proper depth ordering, blending modes, and timing alignment.
04
Audio Integration
Synced background music and sound effects to the visual timeline. Balanced audio levels to complement the rotoscoped motion and pacing of the final video.
05
Text & Typography
Added animated text overlays including titles, subtitles, and motion-tracked labels. Ensured readability and stylistic consistency across all frames.
06
Export & Review
Rendered the final composition at full resolution. Reviewed playback for frame consistency, audio sync, and visual quality before final submission.
// 03 — Documentation

Weekly Progress

Week 1
Research & Planning
Explored rotoscoping techniques and reference videos. Selected the source live-action footage and outlined the overall creative direction of the project. Identified the tools and software to be used.
Research Planning Tool Setup
Week 2
Initial Frame Tracing
Began frame-by-frame rotoscoping of the source footage. Focused on establishing consistent outlines for the subject. Completed approximately the first third of the video sequence.
Rotoscoping Frame Tracing
Week 3
Continued Tracing & Refinement
Continued tracing through the middle section of the footage. Revisited earlier frames for cleanup and consistency. Began experimenting with color and style for the animated overlay layer.
Rotoscoping Cleanup Style Testing
Week 4
Compositing & Audio
Completed tracing of all frames. Composited the rotoscoped layer with background elements. Integrated background audio and sound effects, syncing them to key motion moments in the video.
Compositing Audio Sync Background
Week 5
Text Overlays & Final Export
Added typographic elements including title cards and animated text. Performed final color grading. Exported the completed video at full quality and prepared all project documentation and credits.
Typography Color Grading Final Export
