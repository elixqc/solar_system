Process

A straightforward way to present the work.

The layout follows the design doc: alternating light and dark tiles, quiet blue actions, generous spacing, and typography that stays readable instead of ornamental.

01

Lead with the headline.

The hero tells visitors what the page is about in one pass, with a short subhead and two clear actions.

02

Use the projects as anchors.

The two core pieces sit front and center, with enough metadata to be useful but not enough to crowd the card.

03

Keep the rest lightweight.

Support sections stay concise so the page feels designed, not overexplained. That gives the work room to breathe.
